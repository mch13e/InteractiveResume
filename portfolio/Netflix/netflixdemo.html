Rise of Netflix

Take a look at my first website.

I created this informational website about the creation and growth of Netflix for my Intro to Web Development course. Since I had no previous experience with designing and developing web sites at the beginning of this course, learning the basics of web development (HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Bootstrap) was the biggest challenge that I faced during this project. However, by the end of the project, I had learned how to transform unappealing websites into clean, functional designs, which can be viewed by the drastic differences between the first and final websites that I created for this project.
