Experience

Here's what I've been doing.



Publix Super Markets, Inc. | Customer Service/Cashier

Tallahassee, FL (April 2015 - June 2016)

During my time at Publix, I expanded my customer service skills by greeting and interacting with guests to ensure that their needs were being met. I also gained exceptional teamwork and communication skills by working alongside fellow employees to complete our day-to-day tasks, such as monitoring the flow of cash and providing an inviting store atmosphere by ensuring all products were correctly displayed. The family atmosphere of this company allowed me to enhance my skills in an engaging and supportive work environment, which is one reason I loved working for this company.






TEKsystems | Hardware Installation Technician

Tallahassee, FL (April 2016 - May 2016)

As a part of this short-term project, I worked as a partner with Toshiba and Walmart to install new point-of-sale (POS) systems in various Walmart locations. While there was a small group of five to ten technicians assigned to each Walmart store, we were expected to complete our assigned individual duties, such as upgrading the logistic computers, debit/credit card readers, and weight scales. This experience allowed me to get a feel for what it’s like working with other IT professionals towards completing a specific task in a short amount of time.





Zaxby's | Cashier

Tallahassee, FL (January 2014 - September 2015)

While working at Zaxby’s, I gained first-hand experience in a fast-paced work environment and developed my exceptional time management skills by completing multiple tasks at one time. I also mastered the POS computer system for retail environments and demonstrated leadership skills by assisting with on-the-job training of more than five new hires while at this company.
